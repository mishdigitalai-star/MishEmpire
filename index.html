About
The Woman Behind MisH
Multi-hyphenate creator, social care lead, and AI strategist, with deep roots in health and social care, and a restless drive to build things that matter.

By day, I lead social work teams navigating complex cases. By night, I create AI-powered animated content that resurrects icons, reimagines history, and brings nostalgia to life for a growing community of 31K+ followers.

I'm the creator of Honey Soul, an original music genre fusing Afrobeats groove with Motown soul. I'm building ScribisH.ai, an AI notes platform for the care workforce. And I'm currently completing an Executive MBA in AI with a health and social care specialism.

Former TV presenter and DJ in Nigeria. Former athlete. Always building.
